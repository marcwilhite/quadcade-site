# Quadcade Support

## How to Play

Tap a column to drop the next stone into it. When **four or more** same-value stones touch (4-directionally connected), they merge into the next tier. Chains cascade for exponential score multipliers. Reach **4,096** to WIN, **16,384** for LEGENDARY, and there's one more rung beyond that to discover.

### Combo Meter

Consecutive drops that produce at least one merge build your combo multiplier:

| Streak | Multiplier |
| --- | --- |
| 0 | ×1.0 |
| 1–2 | ×1.5 |
| 3–5 | ×2.0 |
| 6+ | ×3.0 |

A drop that produces no merges shatters the combo back to ×1.

### Named Feats

Certain plays fire a named bonus on the spot:

- **DOUBLE / TRIPLE / QUAD** — two, three, or four merges on a single drop
- **CHAIN-3 / CHAIN-4 / CHAIN-5+** — cascades that reach 3, 4, or 5+ steps deep
- **COMBO MAX** — the first time you hit combo streak 6 in a run
- **PERFECT CLEAR** — the board is completely empty after a drop resolves

Most feats pay a score bonus; the bigger ones also drop a bomb.

### Power-Ups

- **Undo** — revert your last drop. Earned immediately; cools down for 8 drops after use.
- **Wildstone** — closes a cluster of any tier. Tap to arm, then drop it. A sparkles glyph appears on columns where it would merge. Earned at 1,000 and 2,000 score, capped at 2.
- **Bomb** — removes a single stone. Tap the bomb button to arm, then tap any stone on the board. Earned at the 256 milestone, on every 1024 you create, and on QUAD / CHAIN-4+ / PERFECT-CLEAR feats. Cap starts at 2 and grows: 3 after WIN, 4 after LEGENDARY.

### Settings

Tap the **Menu** button (bottom-left) to access:

- **Colorblind Mode** — adds distinct shape overlays to every tier
- **Reduce Motion** — honors the system accessibility setting; can also be toggled per-app
- **Leaderboard** — view the Game Center leaderboard
- **How to Play** — re-read the quick-start anytime
- **Restart Game** — start a fresh run

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I sync my high score across devices?**
Quadcade syncs your high score via iCloud automatically. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID on both devices and that iCloud is enabled for Quadcade in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud.

**How do I see the leaderboard?**
Open the in-game Menu and tap **Leaderboard**. You'll need to be signed in to Game Center.

**What happens if I lose internet mid-run?**
Nothing — Quadcade is fully playable offline. Your high score syncs to iCloud and Game Center the next time you're online.

**The combo badge disappeared. Why?**
Any drop that doesn't produce at least one merge shatters the combo back to ×1. That's the risk/reward tension — keep making merges to keep the multiplier alive.

**Why did my wildstone button light up but then the hint wasn't there?**
The wildstone hint only appears when you **arm** the wildstone by tapping its button. Once armed, columns where it would close a cluster show a magenta glow with a sparkles icon. Tap the column to play it, or tap the button again to cancel.

**Does Quadcade collect any data?**
No. Quadcade collects zero personal data, has no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. Your high score and preferences stay on your device; high scores optionally sync via iCloud and optionally submit to the Game Center leaderboard. See our [Privacy Policy](../privacy/) for full details.

**The game feels too easy / too hard.**
Difficulty tuning is ongoing. Send feedback using the contact info below.
